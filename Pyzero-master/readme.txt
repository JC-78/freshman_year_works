PyZero. It will be a galaxy bullet shooter game. You will be facing waves of enemies and obstacles. 

If you crash into an obstacle, you will lose health; if you get hit by the monster or its bullets, you will also lose health. 

After you kill the waves of monsters, you will confront a midboss and then the final boss. 

You will get to gameover page if your health reaches zero. 

On the other hand, if you manage to destroy the level's boss monster, you clear the level and proceed to next level.

To play PyZero, you need to run 'Final template.py' on Visual Studio Code. You will need to install the following libraries:
1)pygame
2)math
3)random
4)time
5)os
